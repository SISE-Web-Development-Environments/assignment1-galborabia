    The evolution of the web:

    Web1.0
    The Internet is characterized by static read-only pages,
    Most users were content consumers only and the number of content creators was small,
    The information was only updated by the publisher.
    Web2.0
    The Internet is becoming a socio-community place, users are becoming knowledge consumers for knowledge collaborators.
    Web pages become active, users share information and become more active.
    The emergence of social networks and content sharing sites like Wikipedia.
    Web3.0
    The semantic web, integration of information using AI technology.
    The Internet is becoming a source of knowledge in a variety of fields.
    The Internet becomes smart and tailored to the user personally.
